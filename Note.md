📡 New Encryption Algorithm Analysis – Algeria Telecom (HG6145F1)

---

📌 Overview

Hello everyone,

I am currently investigating a recent security update implemented by Algérie Télécom concerning their HG6145F1 optical network terminals (ONTs). This new algorithm is intended to address a previously identified vulnerability in the device's authentication mechanism.

I have observed that the new algorithm differs from its predecessor in how default passwords are generated. This has led me to attempt understanding its inner workings and reverse-engineer the mathematical logic behind it.

---

🎯 Research Objectives

· Understand how default passwords are generated for the new devices.
· Compare the old and new algorithms and identify key differences.
· Release an open-source tool to help users recover lost passwords for their own devices (within a legal and ethical framework).
